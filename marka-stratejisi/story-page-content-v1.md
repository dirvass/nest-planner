### BOLUM s6 -- Bolge Tarihi

**Gorsel onerisi:** Sis icinden gorunen dag silueti veya eski tas yol / toprak patika gorseli

**EN Title:** Roots deeper than ours

**EN Body:**
Ulasli is not new. Caravans passed through these mountains in the medieval age. Roman burial stones still sleep beneath the grass. On one side of the ridge sits Iznik -- where empires held councils and artisans fired cobalt into tiles. On the other, the Gulf of Marmara stretches toward Istanbul. This land has been a crossroads for centuries. We simply added a front door.

**TR Title:** Bizden derin kokler

**TR Body:**
Ulasli yeni degil. Ortacag'da kervanlar bu daglardan gecti. Roma doneminden mezar taslari hala toprağin altinda uyuyor. Sirtlarin bir yaninda Iznik var -- imparatorluklarin konsil topladigi, ustalarin kobalt mavi ciziyi camilere isledigi sehir. Diger yanda Marmara Korfezi Istanbul'a dogru uzaniyor. Bu toprak yuzyillardir bir kavsakti. Biz sadece bir kapi ekledik.

---

### BOLUM s7 -- Verde Etimoloji Derinligi

**Gorsel onerisi:** Yesil yaprak makro / sabah ciyindeki bitki detayi / bahce gorseli

**EN Title:** One word, many tongues

**EN Body:**
In Italian, verde means green. In Arabic, warda means rose -- the word appears in the Quran when the sky splits open like a crimson flower. In the language of gardens, green is what remains when everything else falls away. We chose a name that crosses borders the way colour does: without asking permission.

**TR Title:** Bir kelime, bircok dil

**TR Body:**
Italyanca'da verde yesil demek. Arapca'da warda gul demek -- gokyuzunun kizil bir cicek gibi yarildigini anlatan Kuran ayetinde gecen kelime. Bahcelerin dilinde yesil, geriye kalan her sey dokulunce ayakta kalan seydir. Rengin yaptigi gibi sinir tanimayan bir isim sectik: izin sormadan gecen.

---

### BOLUM s8 -- Agro-Culture Detay

**Gorsel onerisi:** Domates dalinda / eller toprakta / cocuk yumurta toplarken / recel kavanozu masada

**EN Title:** The soil feeds, the hands follow

**EN Body:**
There is a kitchen garden where tomatoes ripen on the vine, peppers grow crooked and honest, and pomegranate trees hold fruit like lanterns. A henhouse delivers eggs still warm each morning. Children plant seeds and return the next visit to find their basil grown tall. Jams are made in season. Olive oil is pressed nearby. The table is set by the land itself.

**TR Title:** Toprak besler, eller eslik eder

**TR Body:**
Bir bostan var; domates dalinda olgunlasir, biberler yamuk ve durust buyur, nar agaclari meyvelerini fener gibi tasir. Her sabah kumes ilik yumurtalarini teslim eder. Cocuklar tohum eker, bir sonraki gelislerinde feslegen boylarini asmis bulur. Receller mevsiminde kavanoza girer. Zeytinyagi yakin koyden gelir. Sofrayi kuran, toprağin kendisidir.

---

### BOLUM s9 -- Doga Deneyimi

**Gorsel onerisi:** Sis icinde orman yolu / dag silueti / 4 mevsim kolaj / yayla gorunumu

**EN Title:** Four seasons, four silences

**EN Body:**
Spring arrives with birdsong and blossoms. Summer lifts the canopy and fills the pool with light. Autumn wraps the hills in mist and delivers the harvest. Winter strips everything bare, leaving only firelight and snow. The altitude here holds a different quiet for each month. Some mornings feel like the Alps. Others feel like nowhere at all.

**TR Title:** Dort mevsim, dort sessizlik

**TR Body:**
Ilkbahar kus sesleri ve ciceklerle gelir. Yaz yapraklari acar, havuzu isikla doldurur. Sonbahar tepeleri sise sarar, hasadi getirir. Kis her seyi soyar, geriye sadece atesi ve kari birakir. Buradaki yukseklik her aya ayri bir sessizlik saklar. Bazi sabahlar Alp eteklerindeymiş gibidir. Bazilari ise hicbir yere ait degilmis gibi.

---

### BOLUM s10 -- Konum

**Gorsel onerisi:** Harita illustrasyonu veya Osmangazi Koprusu manzarasi / drone gorunum

**EN Title:** Close to everything, hidden from all

**EN Body:**
Ski slopes: one hour in winter. Black Sea coves: one hour in summer. Clean Marmara bays: thirty minutes. Sabiha Gokcen Airport: fifty minutes. The heart of Istanbul: seventy minutes. Bursa: seventy minutes. The nearest town for dinner: thirty minutes. Verde sits at the centre of a wheel whose spokes reach mountains, coastlines, and ancient cities. Yet from inside the estate, none of it is visible. Only green. Only quiet.

**TR Title:** Her seye yakin, her seyden gizli

**TR Body:**
Kayak merkezleri: kışın bir saat. Karadeniz koylari: yazin bir saat. Marmara'nin temiz koylari: otuz dakika. Sabiha Gokcen Havalimani: elli dakika. Istanbul'un kalbi: yetmis dakika. Bursa: yetmis dakika. Aksam yemegi icin en yakin ilce: otuz dakika. Verde, kollarini daglara, kiyilara ve tarihi sehirlere uzatan bir tekerlegin tam merkezinde duruyor. Ama arazinin icinden baktiginda hicbiri gorünmez. Sadece yesil. Sadece sessizlik.
